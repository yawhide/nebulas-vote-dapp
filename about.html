About

Mock the ontario election happening right now! For more information about the candidates, please visit the wiki.

How to vote:

  • Select which party you want to vote for. You can choose one of the three parties: PC, NDP, or Lib (for liberals).
  • Click the vote button and follow the promps on the nebulas web extension wallet
  • Wait for the transaction to save your vote on the blockchain. The cost to vote is very very little.
  • If you decide to change your vote, you can simply repeat the first step above!

Requirements

Anyone can vote for the poll with this tool. In order to vote, you will need a small amount of $nas for gas and the WebExtensionWallet available here: https://github.com/ChengOrangeJu/WebExtensionWallet
